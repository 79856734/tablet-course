Photopea
- Overview:
Photopea is a free, web-based photo editor designed to work directly in your browser. It offers many advanced features similar to Adobe Photoshop, allowing you to edit, create, and manipulate images without installing any software.

Purpose:
○ Editing and retouching photographs.
○ Creating web graphics and digital art.
○ Designing marketing materials such as banners and social media posts.
○ Converting and preparing files for print or web use.

Key Features:
○ Layer Support: Work with layers, masks, and blending modes to create complex compositions.
○ PSD Compatibility: Open, edit, and save PSD files along with other popular formats like JPEG, PNG, and SVG.
○ Advanced Tools: Enjoy a wide range of editing tools including selection, clone stamp, healing brush, filters, and adjustment layers.
○ Vector and Raster Graphics: Edit both vector shapes and pixel-based images in a single platform.
○ Seamless Photoshop Integration: Photopea’s interface and feature set closely mimic Adobe Photoshop, enabling users to effortlessly transfer their Photoshop skills and workflows. This similarity makes transitioning between Photopea and Photoshop smooth, preserving familiarity and efficiency.
